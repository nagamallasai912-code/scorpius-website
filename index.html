How It Works

Our four-step autonomous process transforms your IT operations through intelligent automation

Understand

Ingests and integrates data from diverse sources to build a comprehensive operational context. This enables the system to 'see' the environment holistically and accurately.

Reason

Analyzes contextual data, formulates hypotheses, draws inferences, and identifies cause-effect relationships to guide decision-making.

Take Action

Interacts with software and infrastructure tools to propose or implement changes—closing the loop between analysis and operational execution.

Learn

Continuously evolves by observing the outcomes of its actions, integrating human feedback, and refining its internal models to improve over time.
